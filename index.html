Measuring success

User Involvement
It is important that the users of the completed product are properly involved in the design and useability of the software. This ensures they have buy-in once the product is released.

Executive Management Support
Your project will gain the most traction if all stakeholders are involved. But none more so than your executive management. By ensuring your management are supportive of the change, I can ensure the project meets their needs and yours.

Clear Statement Of Requirements
No project is successful without a clear understanding of all requirements. That is why before any development begins, an agreement of what the requirements are viewed and agreed upon by your team first.
